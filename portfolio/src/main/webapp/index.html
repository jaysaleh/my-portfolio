Mexico Trip

After wrapping up freshman year, my high school friends and I reunited in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico for about 9 days. We spent most of our time in Mexico relaxing on the beach, exploring nearby cities, trying different foods, and snorkeling. My favorite restaurant during this trip was the Sonora Grill Prime.
